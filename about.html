About Concerts Promotion

Concerts Promotion is growing to become the world's largest concert search engine. We're dedicated to helping live music fans more easily and quickly find concert tickets and information about their favourite artists and venues. We're constantly rolling out new features to improve our concert search experience. Many of these are in response to suggestions from fans like you, so if you have a question or comment please let us know.
